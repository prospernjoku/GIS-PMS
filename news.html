Ministry Announcements

New Initiatives to Boost Affordable Housing

The Ministry of Housing and Urban Development announces a series of new initiatives aimed at increasing the availability of affordable housing options for citizens. These include partnerships with private developers, streamlined approval processes, and financial incentives for sustainable building practices.

Revised Regulations for Property Development

The Ministry has released updated regulations for property development, focusing on environmental sustainability and urban planning. These changes aim to ensure responsible growth and enhance the quality of life in urban areas.
